Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know coding to join?

Not at all! We recruit based on passion and willingness to learn. We have a 'Beginner' track specifically for first-year students.

How much is the membership fee?

Membership is completely free for all TRCAC students.

Can Commerce/Arts students join?

Yes! We need designers, content writers, and managers. If you have an interest in tech, you are welcome.
